# Wheedle Technologies

> Wheedle Technologies is an AI-first technology company headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India (incorporated 2017). The company builds and deploys autonomous AI agents and agentic platforms across web engineering, app development, software, IT consulting, digital marketing, graphic design, UI/UX, and intelligent automation — helping businesses replace manual workflows with self-operating AI systems.
